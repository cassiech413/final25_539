Vocaloid Milestone Songs (2007-2016)

2007

みくみくにしてあげる♪ (Miku Miku ni Shite Ageru♪)

ika × 初音ミク

One of the first songs to make Hatsune Miku go viral on NicoNico Douga and establish her as an internet icon.

2008

ココロ (Kokoro)

トラボルタ × 鏡音リン

A landmark early ballad that showed Vocaloid could carry emotional, story-driven songs rather than just meme tracks.

2009

ロミオとシンデレラ (Romeo and Cinderella)

doriko × 初音ミク

A hugely popular love-themed song that became a long-lasting favorite in the community and helped define “classic Miku pop”.

2010

ローリンガール (Rolling Girl)

wowaka × 初音ミク

A raw emotional rock track whose chaotic energy and lyrics influenced an entire generation of Vocaloid “emo rock” songs.

2011

千本桜 (Senbonzakura)

黒うさP × 初音ミク

A cultural phenomenon that mixed traditional Japanese motifs with rock, becoming one of the most recognizable Vocaloid songs worldwide.

2012

Tell Your World

kz × 初音ミク

Featured in a Google Chrome commercial, this song helped introduce Vocaloid to mainstream audiences and captured the feeling of creating and sharing online.

2013

裏表ラバーズ (Ura-Omote Lovers)

wowaka × 初音ミク

A fast, intense track that became a staple in live performances and showcased how far technical and vocal expression had evolved.

2014

ウミユリ海底譚 (Umiyuri Kaiteitan)

n-buna × 初音ミク

A poetic mid-2010s song that blends gentle rock, atmospheric storytelling, and emotional lyrics, representing a more “indie” Vocaloid sound.

2015

ドーナツホール (Doughnut Hole)

ハチ × GUMI

Known for its striking visuals and alternative rock sound, this track pushed a more experimental, band-like direction for Vocaloid music.

2016

ゴーストルール (Ghost Rule)

DECO*27 × 初音ミク

A modern powerhouse hit with intense vocal expression and electro-rock style, often seen as a symbol of the mature, late-era Vocaloid sound.
