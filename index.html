Welcome to the Vocaloid World

Exploring digital voices and culture surrounding them.

Vocaloid is a singing voice synthesis technology developed by Yamaha that enables creators to produce music using virtual voicebanks such as 初音ミク (Hatsune Miku), 鏡音リン・レン (Kagamine Rin/Len), GUMI, and 巡音ルカ (Megurine Luka).
Since its release in the mid-2000s, Vocaloid has evolved from a production tool into a global creative ecosystem supported by composers, illustrators, animators, and online communities. It continues to influence digital music trends, virtual performance, and fan-driven culture across video platforms like Niconico and YouTube.
