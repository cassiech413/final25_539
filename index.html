Welcome To Vocaloid World

Vocaloid & J-Pop Music Archive

A curated digital space for exploring the sound, aesthetics, and cultural impact of Vocaloid and J-pop.

Vocaloid is a voice-synthesis technology developed by Yamaha that enables creators to produce music using virtual singers such as 初音ミク (Hatsune Miku), 鏡音リン・レン (Kagamine Rin/Len), 巡音ルカ (Megurine Luka), and others. Over time, it has grown into a global creative culture that shapes online music, illustration, animation, and fan communities.
